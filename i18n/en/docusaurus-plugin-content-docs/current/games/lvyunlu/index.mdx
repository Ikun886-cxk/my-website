## <i className="fa-duotone fa-badminton"></i> Game Introduction

### <i className="fa-duotone fa-cloud-meatball"></i> Story Background
  
<Typography variant="subtitle2" gutterBottom component="div">
	  The feudal kingdom "Wan Yan" is declining in power, and the feudal kingdoms are in a state of war. Eleven years ago, the small country of Pali on the East Sea was destroyed by the great northern kingdom of Slaughter. Eleven years later, on the outskirts of the former capital of Pali, Prince Araju of Pali came with reinforcements and prepared to take back his country. In the wargame "Elysium Above", players will act as commanders and witness the rise and fall of the East China Sea countries together with the eight protagonists.
	  </Typography>

<Typography variant="subtitle2" gutterBottom component="div">
	  In Elysium Above, players will play the role of a small country by the East China Sea - "Pal" the junior commander, in the mountain rain, dark clouds pressed the city through clever military formations and superb command to help the newly restored Pal country to resist the encroachment of strong enemies around, step by step Explore the demands of the various forces and finally make a choice to lead the story to a different direction. The game uses a semi-instant "moment" system. Characters can move and use skills at will until they run out of action points; however, the more action points you consume, the slower your next action will come. Pay close attention to the action order bar and focus on destroying enemies. By combining different active and passive skills, the same teammate can appear in a completely different position. Recruit new teammates and improve your understanding with them through perfect level breaking, chapter break activities, completing branch lines and various decisions to gain various benefits. Move around the Pali country and collect dozens of treasures to strengthen your character. Important items may affect the ending.
	  </Typography>	  

### <i className="fa-duotone fa-address-card"></i> About World Wanyan	  
	
<Typography variant="subtitle2" gutterBottom component="div">
		  In the continent, a feudal state that has been in existence for thousands of years, the "World State of Yan", has inevitably come to the brink of disintegration after enjoying a long period of peace. The suzerainty, the prestige of World Yan, has been declining since 800 years ago, and the various feudal states have been fighting against each other, devouring countless countries and lives in war. The traditional military power, Killing, has been annexing surrounding countries for a hundred years and is now the leading and most powerful power in the land. The newly formed Seven Nation Alliance, under the leadership of the alliance leader Hong Nation, is in opposition to the Killing Nation and is doing everything it can to bring in more nations to join the alliance. In the middle of the two powers is the main stage of the game - the Pali Kingdom. The Pali Kingdom was destroyed by a surprise attack by the Killing State eleven years ago, and the king, Kui, was martyred by fire in the royal palace - Wan Yang Palace, and the first prince, Ara Zhu, ran away with the third prince to the Hong State in the south. After that, the old palace territory was occupied by the Killing Kingdom. Eleven years later, in order to restore the country, the first prince Araju took advantage of the slackness of the occupying army to lead the army to kill back to the old Pali territory, and the shadow of war and death once again loomed over the East Sea coast. This war of restoration may just be the beginning of a series of chaos ......
	  </Typography>
